Who We Are

Acutus is your best partner in IT Services, consulting, training and business solutions. With a passion in Web development and delivering Quality services, we have strong team of skilled and experienced IT experts. We have dedicated team to handle clients of all sizes, ranging from small to large enterprises with equal importance.

What We Do

Acutus is committed to deliver comprehensive Web development, maintenance, Quality Assurance and Training services. We develop web applications ranging from custom website design to complex enterprise applications. We have talented professionals with vast business domain knowledge and technology competence and proven methodologies to deliver high-quality, cost-effective results.

Our Philosophy

Vision
To become preferred, one stop vendor to provide solutions for all phases of software life cycle.
Mission
is delivering maximum value to our clients to help them succeed in a constantly changing and challenging business world. Our fundamental corporate values stem from understanding that our success is tied to the success of our clients.

Our Strengths

Experience and Expertise
Acutus has built a large knowledge base that enables us to deliver the right solutions to address clients' needs. Acutus IT professionals are highly skilled and experienced in providing Web application development services and solutions and having completed hundreds of projects in different domains.

Acutus Quality Initiatives are based on a 3Q approach: Quality Personnel, Quality Process and Quality Communication. Each project at Acutus is under the constant command of a dedicated quality assurance team that monitors all project activities and results at each phase.

Acutus has a vast pool of IT experts from which to select specialists whose background and skill set best meets client project specifications. Our resource pool includes business analysts, project managers, software architects, Web and graphic designers, flash animators, software developers, quality assurance and testing specialists.
